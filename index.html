01 — What is Neuron

A living brain
for your company.

Every company runs on invisible knowledge — how refunds get handled, who approves pricing exceptions, what the oncall runbook actually says. It lives in Slack threads, outdated docs, and people's heads. When a key person leaves, that knowledge disappears permanently.

Neuron connects to the tools your team already uses, reads everything, and extracts the structured rules and decisions behind your work — keeping them verified and instantly accessible to humans and AI agents alike. Not a search tool. Not a chatbot. A Company Brain.

02 — The Problem

The real cost of
broken knowledge.

These are not theoretical problems. They happen every day in every company — and they compound silently.

🧠
Problem 01

Knowledge lives in people's heads

Every rule and exception lives partly in someone's memory. When that person leaves — you find out the hard way what you didn't know you were missing.

🔄
Problem 02

Onboarding takes months instead of days

New hires spend 3–6 months piecing together how your company works — interrupting teammates, reading outdated docs, and still getting things wrong.

🚪
Problem 03

When someone leaves, their knowledge leaves too

That engineer who knew why the system was built that way. That support lead who handled edge cases nobody documented. Gone in two weeks notice, forever.

🤖
Problem 04

AI agents hallucinate company facts

Your AI agents give confidently wrong answers about your actual policies — because they have no reliable access to how your company really works.
