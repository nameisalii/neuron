## What is Neuron?

Every company runs on invisible knowledge. How refunds get handled. Who approves pricing exceptions. What the oncall runbook actually says. This knowledge lives in Slack threads, outdated docs, and people's heads.

When someone new joins, they spend months piecing it together. When a key person leaves, that knowledge disappears permanently. And when you deploy AI agents to automate work, they fail — because they have no reliable context about how your company actually operates.

**Neuron is the missing layer.** It connects to the tools your team already uses, reads everything, extracts the structured rules and decisions behind your company's work, and keeps them verified, current, and instantly accessible — to humans and AI agents alike.

Not a search tool. Not a chatbot. A Company Brain.

## What Neuron Does

### 🧠 Core Knowledge Engine
Connects to Slack, Notion, GitHub, Google Drive, and Jira. Reads everything. Extracts structured rules and decisions — not just documents. Keeps them verified, timestamped, and conflict-free.

### 🐛 Real-time Bug Detection
Watches connected channels. When a bug is mentioned, it identifies the owner and sends a quiet, contextual alert — no manual tagging required.

### 📋 Implicit Task Extraction
"Someone remind John to finish the API migration by 5pm" → Neuron creates the task, assigns it, and sets the reminder automatically.

### 🧬 Clone Agent
Over time, Neuron learns how each person works — coding patterns, decision style, how they handle edge cases. When they leave, their clone agent stays. New hires inherit full context from day one.

### 💡 Idea Capture
Good ideas die in Slack threads. Neuron catches them, saves them to a structured log, cross-references them against your company OKRs, and suggests improvements based on what's been tried before.

### ✅ Instant Verified Answers
Any team member can ask any question about how the company works and get a verified, sourced answer in under one second. Not a list of documents. A sourced fact — with owner and timestamp.

### ⚠️ Conflict Detection
When two sources say different things, Neuron catches it before anyone acts on wrong information. The rule is frozen, the right people are pinged, and the knowledge base updates the moment someone confirms.
